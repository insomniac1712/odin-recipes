Dalgona Coffee

The recipe for a refreshing cup of hot coffee!

You wake up in the morning and start your day with an aromatic, earthy and delicious brew of coffee. Perfect way to start your day!

Keep it simple

The key to make a beautiful cup of coffee is to keep it simple

Use good quality ingredients and make yourself a power packed brew!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of instant coffee powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons of hot water
  • 1 cup of milk (hot or cold, depending on your preference)
  • A pinch of cinnamon powder (optional, for garnish)
  • A pinch of instant coffee powder (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

Prepare the coffee mixture

  • In a small bowl, combine the instant coffee powder, sugar, and hot water..
  • Using a spoon or a small whisk, beat the mixture vigorously until it becomes light and frothy. This should take about 2-3 minutes..

Heat the milk

  • If you prefer hot coffee, heat the milk in a saucepan until it is warm but not boiling.
  • If you prefer cold coffee, you can use chilled milk.

Assemble the coffee

  • Pour the milk into a cup.
  • Spoon the frothy coffee mixture on top of the milk.
  • Stir gently to combine.

Garnish and serve!

  • Sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon powder or instant coffee powder on top for extra flavor.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious cup of coffee!

This method, often referred to as “beaten coffee” or “Dalgona coffee,” is popular for its creamy texture and rich flavor. Do you have a favorite way to enjoy your coffee? ☕
